Validate submissions

Once a submission is made by a contestant, it is automatically evaluated by the judging system, it is numbered and appears in the overall submissions as well as in the pendings list.

For the person judges to validate a submission they must do the following:

  1. Open the submission report: this is done by clicking on the link associated with the submission number.

  2. Compare obtained and expected results: the report shows, side by side, the results that were exepected and those obtained with the submitted program. By looking at these, the judges can confirm the submission and making it final, or else to revise the classification made by the system.
